What is Accessibility?

TL:DR - Web Accessibility means that people of all abilities can access your Web site

Diversity of Abilities

  • Auditory/Hearing
  • Cognitive/Neurological
  • Physical/Motor
  • Speech
  • Blindness/Low Vision

Why should I care?

The basics

Section 508 Complaince

"Section 508 requires that all website content be accessible to people with disabilities. This applies to Web applications, Web pages and all attached files on the intranet, as well as, internet."

-HHS.gov

WCAG

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines

3 levels:

  • WCAG A (bare minimum)
  • AA (happy medium)
  • AAA (highest)

WCAG Principles

  • Sites should be Perceivable to multiple senses.
  • Sites should be Operable using a mouse, keyboard, or assitive device.
  • Sites should have clear content and be Understandable.
  • Sites should support a Robust variety of browsers.
